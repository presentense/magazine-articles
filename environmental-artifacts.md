<p>Our roads and infrastructure are the essence of our modern, man-made environment. When&nbsp;a field is paved over, or a forest is knocked down, oftentimes it&rsquo;s to put a road through&mdash;or back in the day, to build a railroad. The disturbance of nature so that humans may have ease of&nbsp;transport is the essence of the images and poetry that follow.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Artifacts</strong></p>
<p>Here&rsquo;s some trash</p>
<p>from one of those</p>
<p>civilizations</p>
<p>I&rsquo;ve heard so much</p>
<p>about</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>whose plastics</p>
<p>will last</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>just as long</p>
<p>as their steel</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>yes it&rsquo;s true</p>
<p>that little bit</p>
<p>blowing by your</p>
<p>feet right now</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>will still be</p>
<p>around after</p>
<p>you pass</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>it doesn&rsquo;t matter</p>
<p>how long</p>
<p>you last</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>faces and names</p>
<p>are always</p>
<p>set to change</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>but the trash</p>
<p>just keeps on</p>
<p>being trash</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Inside Ride</strong></p>
<p>Glad I&rsquo;m not</p>
<p>on the roof</p>
<p>of this</p>
<p>thing</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>I would have</p>
<p>needed</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>a few extra</p>
<p>layers</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>I didn&rsquo;t bring</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>Get a load</p>
<p>of this road</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>it&rsquo;s been</p>
<p>paved in</p>
<p>code</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>take a left</p>
<p>no a right</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>hug that</p>
<p>curve tight</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>grab the map</p>
<p>do the math</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>steer us quickly</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>closer to</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>smoother</p>
<p>paths.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Forever Red</strong></p>
<p>The red and the</p>
<p>yellow and</p>
<p>the green</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>command</p>
<p>motorized armies</p>
<p>taking orders</p>
<p>from a hue</p>
<p>and sheen</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>everyone reacts</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>it&rsquo;s just</p>
<p>one of</p>
<p>those facts</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>looking at</p>
<p>lights</p>
<p>we every day</p>
<p>try</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>to chase</p>
<p>down</p>
<p>but before</p>
<p>we go back</p>
<p>to</p>
<p>cruising around</p>
<p>this town</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>let&rsquo;s do something</p>
<p>novel instead</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>let&rsquo;s save ourselves</p>
<p>an ozone</p>
<p>layer</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>and turn</p>
<p>all those traffic</p>
<p>lights</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>forever red.</p>
